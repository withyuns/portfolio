Project

Database Development & Analytics

Kims Market

A market database system that handles numerous data in DB, and analyzes data trends such as similar product pairs and top sales based on filters such as category and state. The main features of this app include insert/update/remove data, and also transactions between carts and orders from customers. This app also was developed with efficient performances such as cost and speed. A major purpose for this app is to interact between server-side and client-side. Finally I updated real time data using Ajax call.

Our app has two roles - customers and owners. Owners can create categories, products, but customers cannot. Owners can also create/update/delete categories/products. One of the challenging decisions in this project was to decide whether deletion is hard-deletion or soft-deletion. The purpose for this app is not only a transaction but also data analysis. We chose soft-deletion, because we are not losing any data information after purchasing.
